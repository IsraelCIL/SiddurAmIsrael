**Description (≤4000):**
```
Siddur Am Israel is a complete, offline-first Orthodox siddur that always shows the correct prayer for the moment — the right service, nusach, and halachic additions for today's date and time. No internet connection needed, anywhere in the world.

KEY FEATURES

• Three nusachim — Ashkenaz, Sfard, and Edot HaMizrach
• Daily services — Shacharit, Mincha, and Maariv, selected automatically by the halachic times of day (zmanim)
• Full Jewish calendar — Shabbat, Yom Tov, Chol HaMoed, Rosh Chodesh, Chanukah, Purim, fast days, Sefirat HaOmer, Sukkot (Hoshanot and the daily Korban), and more
• Torah reading — Mondays & Thursdays, Rosh Chodesh, Chol HaMoed, Chanukah, Purim, and fast days
• Smart additions — the app inserts the right text automatically (Ya'aleh VeYavo, Al HaNisim, the Omer count, Tachanun, and more) based on the day
• Personal settings — nusach, men's and women's text per halacha, Israel or Diaspora, praying with a minyan or alone
• Easy navigation — jump to any section within a service
• Adjustable font size — clear, fully vowelized (nikud) Hebrew text
• 100% offline — no account, no ads, no tracking, no data collected

Siddur Am Israel follows strictly Orthodox halachic practice throughout.

May your prayers be answered.
```
